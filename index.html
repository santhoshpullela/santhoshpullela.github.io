Roster Mapping Process

Roster Mapping process is divided into two job streams Roster Mapping-1 (RM1) and Roster Mapping-2(RM2) with the intention that the contribution related infroamtion to be processed first and all the necessary information of a participant to be processed at a later time, resulting in reduction of processing time.
